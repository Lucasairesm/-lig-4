Tic-Tac-Toe

Objective

The first player to align 3 consecutive pieces (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

Board

The board is a vertical grid of 3 columns by 3 rows.

Pieces

Each player has a piece of a distinct color and figure.

Rules

  • Players take turns, with each player filling an empty space with their colored piece in any space on the board.
  • The game continues until one player aligns 3 consecutive pieces, or until the board is completely filled, resulting in a draw.

End of Game

The game ends when one player successfully aligns 3 pieces, and the winner is declared based on the color chosen by the player. At the end, the player must click the button to end the game and start a new one.

In the event of a draw, players will have the option to restart the game or exit.
